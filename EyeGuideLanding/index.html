Your AI Eyes, Always Ready

Four powerful modes designed for real-world independence, powered by advanced AI vision.

Scene Description

Point your camera and hear a natural description of your surroundings. EyeGuide identifies obstacles, pathways, vehicles, and landmarks to help you navigate safely.

Text Reading

Read signs, menus, mail, and documents aloud. Simply capture an image and EyeGuide reads every word in natural, conversational speech.

Object Finder

Looking for your keys, phone, or the door? Tell EyeGuide what you need and it guides you with directional cues — left, right, ahead — and approximate distance.

Social Assist

Understand social situations with confidence. EyeGuide describes people around you — how many, what they are doing, their expressions and body language.

Simple to Use, Powerful to Experience

Get started in seconds. No complex setup required.

1

Open the App

Launch EyeGuide on your iPhone or Android device. The AI assistant activates automatically and announces it is ready.

2

Choose a Mode

Tap the Voice Guide button or select a feature — Scene, Read, Locate, or Social. Each mode is optimized for a specific task.

3

Hear the World

Point your camera and receive instant audio descriptions. EyeGuide speaks naturally, like a friend guiding you through your day.

Powered by Advanced AI

EyeGuide leverages Google Gemini AI for real-time visual understanding. The AI processes camera images instantly and responds in natural, conversational language — no robotic text-to-speech.

  • Real-time voice guidance with live camera streaming
  • Bilingual support — English and Chinese
  • Adjustable detail levels — concise, standard, or detailed descriptions
  • Conversational AI that remembers context within a session
